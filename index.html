Colonel Robert Green Ingersoll

The Great Agnostic

SUMMARY

One of the most prolific orators of 19th century America but little known by modern society.

Significant events in the life of Robert G. Ingersoll

  • 1833 - Born in Dresden, New York to an abolitionist Presbyterian minister father.
  • 1854 - Apprenticed under two lawyers thereby being admitted to the bar in Illinois
  • 1861 - Raised and lead the 11th Regiment Illinois Volunteer Calvary for the Union Army
  • 1862 - Married Eva Amelia Parker on Valentines Day
  • 1867 - Appointed the first Attorney General of Illinois
  • 1899 - Died of congestive heart failure in Dobbs Ferry, NY
